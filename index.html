Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026
App: CaptionCraft (com.captioncraft.ai)
Version: 1.0.0

CaptionCraft (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides a mobile and desktop application that helps creators generate captions, subtitles, and content insights for videos and podcasts. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using CaptionCraft.

1. Summary

  • Your media stays yours. Video and audio files are processed on your device or sent directly to AI providers you choose using your own API keys.
  • Bring Your Own Key (BYOK). Transcription requests go from your device to the AI provider you configure — not through our servers for processing.
  • Minimal account data. If you sign in, we store basic profile information through our authentication provider.
  • Optional analytics. We may collect usage events to improve the app when analytics is enabled.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account information — email address, display name, and profile photo when you create an account or sign in (e.g. via email or Google).
  • AI provider credentials — API keys and related settings (such as Azure region) that you enter to connect AssemblyAI, ElevenLabs, Azure Speech, Sarvam AI, OpenAI Whisper, Deepgram, Gladia, Groq, or other supported providers.
  • App preferences — default language, export format, output mode, and similar settings.
  • Project data — caption text, subtitles, project names, transcription metadata, and content analysis results you generate within the app.
  • Team workspace data — if you use team features, member information and shared configuration may be stored in our cloud backend.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage analytics — events such as app opened, file uploaded, transcription started/completed, export triggered, and subscription actions (when PostHog analytics is configured).
  • Subscription status — plan tier and purchase information processed through RevenueCat and the relevant app store (Google Play, Apple App Store).
  • Device & app data — basic technical information needed for the app to function (e.g. platform, app version, network connectivity).

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not store your raw video or audio files on our servers for transcription.
  • We do not collect or store payment card numbers — payments are handled by the app store or RevenueCat.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide caption generation, transcription, export, and content analysis features.
  • Store your projects and preferences locally and, where enabled, sync team data.
  • Authenticate your account and manage team workspaces.
  • Process subscriptions and verify your plan tier.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience through analytics.
  • Respond to support requests and comply with legal obligations.

4. How Transcription & AI Processing Works

CaptionCraft uses a Bring Your Own Key model. When you transcribe media, audio is extracted on your device (using FFmpeg where needed) and sent directly from your device to the third-party AI provider you selected, using the API key you supplied.

Those providers process your audio according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any provider you connect, including:

  • AssemblyAI
  • ElevenLabs
  • Microsoft (Azure Speech)
  • OpenAI
  • Deepgram
  • Other providers you choose to connect within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

CaptionCraft may integrate with the following categories of third-party services:

  • AI transcription providers — process audio you submit using your API keys (see Section 4).
  • Supabase — authentication, user profiles, and team workspace data (when cloud features are enabled).
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and entitlement verification.
  • PostHog — product analytics (only when an analytics key is configured at build time).
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — in-app purchases and subscription billing.

Each third party processes data under its own terms and privacy policy. We only share the minimum information required for each integration to work.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • API keys are stored using platform secure storage (encrypted on device).
  • Projects and captions are stored locally on your device using on-device databases.
  • Account and team data may be stored in Supabase when you use sign-in or team features.
  • We use HTTPS for network communications and industry-standard safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Permissions

Depending on your platform, CaptionCraft may request:

  • Storage / media access — to import video and audio files for captioning.
  • Microphone — if you record or capture audio within the app.
  • Internet — to connect to AI providers, authentication, subscriptions, and analytics.

You can revoke permissions in your device settings, though some features may stop working.

8. Data Retention

  • Local project data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • API keys remain in secure storage until you remove them or clear app data.
  • Account data is retained while your account is active and as needed to provide the service.
  • Analytics data is retained according to our analytics provider’s policies.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Export your project data from within the app.
  • Disconnect AI providers and delete stored API keys at any time.
  • Cancel your subscription through the app store where you purchased it.
  • Request account deletion by contacting us (see Section 12).

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, additional rights may apply under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA/CPRA. Contact us to exercise those rights.

10. Children’s Privacy

CaptionCraft is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate (e.g. the United States). We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place when data is transferred internationally.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Effective date” at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of CaptionCraft after an update means you accept the revised policy.
